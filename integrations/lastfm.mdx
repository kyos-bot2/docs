## **What is [Last.FM](http://Last.FM)?**

Last.FM is a website which tracks the music you listen to and provides statistics based on your listening history. You can register for a [Last.FM](http://Last.FM) account by clicking [**here**](https://www.last.fm/join).

**Creating a [Last.FM](http://Last.FM) account**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register">
    Go to the [**Last.FM**](http://Last.FM)**[ registration page](https://www.last.fm/join)** and fill in info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your [inbox](https://gmail.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Linking your Spotify">
    To effectively track ur music, we suggest linking your [**Spotify**](https://spotify.com/) account view the **Applications** tab in your account settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Connecting your [Last.FM](http://Last.FM) account**

Use the`lastfm login`command to set account, after this kyos will automatically recognise it as your account.
